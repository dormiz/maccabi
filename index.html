About the club

Maccabi Haifa Football Club is an Israeli professional football club, based in the city of Haifa, a section of Maccabi Haifa sports club. The club plays in the Israeli Premier League. Maccabi Haifa home games are played at Sammy Ofer Stadium. The stadium, which is shared with rivals Hapoel Haifa, is the second largest in Israel football, with a capacity of 30,780


Maccabi Haifa is one of the "Big Four" clubs in Israeli football. The side has won thirteen league titles, six State Cups and four Toto Cups. Maccabi has won the championship and the cup in the same season (referred to as winning the "double") once, and was the first Israeli club to qualify for the group stage of the UEFA Champions League.
